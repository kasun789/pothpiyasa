General Guidlines

  • Silence should be observed in the library.

  • Seats in the Library should not be reserved by anyone.

  • Readers are not allowed to do joint work in the reading areas.

  • Readers are allowed to remove any book they require from the shelves for use within the Library.

  • Readers should not replace any book on the shelves after using them but should hand them over to a member of the Library staff or leave them on the table provided.

  • Readers should not use Mobile Phones in the library.

  • No bags, cases, personal books or any such belongings should be brought in to the library. Only blank papers and printed paper notes are allowed

  • Consumption of food or drinks/water is forbidden in the Library. Smoking is forbidden in the UCSC building complex.

  • Bottles of ink or ink wells should not be brought in to the Library.

  • Those who are not registered to use this Library are required to obtain permission from the Director of UCSC to use the Library.

Readers must produce their University Identity Card when borrowing Books and at any time when called upon to do so.
