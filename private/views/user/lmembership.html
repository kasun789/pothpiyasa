Membership

  • Staff of UCSC, and DST of the University of Colombo, UCSC students, registered for Undergraduate and Postgraduate courses.

  • Other persons, for purpose of Special Study, approved at the discretion of the Director of UCSC on a case by case basis.

Issue of Books

Books are issued on loan for a period of two weeks and may be borrowed and returned any time during the hours of operation. Users must produce their identity cards issued by the UCSC on each occasion they need to borrow books from the Library.

Borrowing

Undergraduates : Two Books

Postgraduates : Two Books

Masters Courses (Computing) : Two Book

Research Students (Computing) : Two Books

Members can use their UCSC identity card as their library ticket.
