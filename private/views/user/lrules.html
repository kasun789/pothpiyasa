General Rules on The Use Of Library

  • Silence should be maintained in the library.

  • Seats of the Library should not be reserved by anyone.

  • Readers are not allowed to do joint works in the reading areas.

  • Readers are allowed to remove any book that they require from the shelves for their use within the Library area.

  • Readers are not permitted to replace any book on the shelves after using them but should hand them over to a member of the Library staff or leave them on the provided table

  • Readers are not allowed to use Mobile Phones in the library.

  • Bags, cases, personal books or any such belongings cannot be brought into the Library. Only blank papers and printed paper notes are allowed.

  • Consumption of food or drinks/water is forbidden in the Library. Smoking is forbidden in the UCSC building complex.

  • The Library will not accept any correspondence or telephone calls on behalf of readers.

Readers must produce their University Identity Card when borrowing Books and at any time when called upon to do so.
