Reference Reading Rooms

UCSC library provides two special reference rooms. More than fifty students can use this area. Students are allowed to bring their laptop to reference rooms. Free internet access through WF-Fi is provided for all users.

General Reading Rooms

Students can discuss and bring their notes to this area.

Past Paper Collection

Undergraduate and postgraduate past papers are available in this collection.

User Education

All the new library members (students and staff) are introduced to the library’ wealth of resources, facilities, and services.

Provide a basic idea on the library’s online public access catalog (OPAC). Students are guided to different kinds of electronic information resources in the UCSC digital library.
